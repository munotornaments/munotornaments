About our Shop

  • Our vision : To be a highly efficient and globally respected firm with an emphasis on client care and a culture of professional ethics.

Munot Ornaments Annex, a 25year old organization which deals in crafting of ethnic and antique gold based jewelry. We keep upgrading our range from time to time to meet the demand of our end consumer and B2B Retailers from all over India. Along with the design, concepts and authenticity of the product we also believe that selling product is not only the key metrics if social values are not been taken care off. We have achieved tremendous reputation in terms of genuine work and commitment in the industry. Under the visionary leadership of our Founder and proprietor Mr. Hukamraj .H.
